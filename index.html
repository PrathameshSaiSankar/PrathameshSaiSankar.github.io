Ireland Tamil Sangam

Ireland Tamil Sangam’s mission is to identify all Tamils, to associate and integrate them with us and act as a shelter to promote the well-being of the community with the support of all people. It's aim is to perpetuate, integrate, and assimilate the cultural heritage of Tamil speaking people into Irish culture.

We Care.

Our goal is to support all Tamil people in Ireland.
