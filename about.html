Who we are

Ireland Tamil Sangam started on Tamil New Year’s day in 2018 with a strong drive to serve for the welfare of Tamils living across all counties in Ireland to grow as a community ethically sound with societal awareness, highest quality of health and well-being. It is registered in Ireland as an entity and expected to get non-profit organization status sometime in 2019. Sangam’s mission is to identify all Tamils, to associate and integrate them with the Sangam and act as a shelter to promote the well being of the community with the support of all people. Its aim is to perpetuate, integrate, and assimilate the cultural heritage of the Tamil speaking people into the mainstream of Irish.
